How to Print Your ID Card
  1. Download the PDF file by clicking the "Download PDF" button above.
  2. Open the PDF file with Adobe Reader or your default PDF viewer.
  3. Print on CR80 cardstock (85.6mm × 53.98mm) at 100% scale.
  4. Make sure "Fit to page" is disabled to maintain the correct size.
  5. Cut along the edges using a card cutter for clean cuts.
  6. You now have your official student ID card!
