How to Apply
  1. Purchase an official KNUST application e-voucher.
  2. Register and log in using your voucher PIN and serial number.
  3. Select the appropriate entry mode and programme.
  4. Upload all required academic and identification documents.
  5. Review and submit the completed application before the deadline.
  6. Print application summary where required and submit by post if instructed.
  7. Check the portal regularly for admission decisions and notices.
Required Documents & Notices
  • Relevant examination results or academic transcripts
  • Certified certificates (HND / Degree where applicable)
  • Passport-sized photograph and valid photo identification
  • Programme-specific supporting documents
  • False declarations may result in disqualification
Applicants are advised to review payment instructions, deadlines and official announcements carefully. Incomplete applications will not be processed.
